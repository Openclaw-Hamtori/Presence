# Presence Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-27

Presence is designed to support device linking and presence verification flows.

## Information We Process

Depending on how the app is used, Presence may process:

- device-related identifiers used for linking and verification
- presence verification records and request status
- activity- or health-related signals made available through Apple Health / HealthKit, such as step count and heart rate, when permission is granted by the user
- basic diagnostic information needed to operate and secure the service

## How Information Is Used

Presence uses this information to:

- link a device to a service account
- evaluate and submit presence verification results
- maintain verification history and request state
- improve reliability, security, and abuse prevention

## Health Data

If you grant HealthKit access, Presence may read limited health/activity data required for presence verification flows. This data is used only to support app functionality and is not used for advertising.

## Data Sharing

Presence does not sell personal information. Data may be transmitted to the service endpoints used by the app in order to complete linking, verification, and related security checks.

## Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, maintain security, and support verification workflows.

## Your Choices

You can choose whether to grant access to HealthKit data. If you do not grant required permissions, some verification features may not function.
